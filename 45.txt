Job Description:

A postdoctoral position is available starting immediately in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University, 
under the supervision  of Prof. Sunil Prabhakar.  The position is limited term and can be extended for one or more 
additional years by mutual consent. 

Individuals with research interests in the areas of data management, including big data, data security and privacy, uncertain 
databases, and graph databases are invited to submit applications. Applicants should have clearly demonstrated skills and research experience
in the area of databases.  
Responsibilities include research on the projects, helping prepare proposals, publications, and working with graduate students. Teaching responsibilities are optional.  

Review of applications will begin immediately and will continue until the position is filled.  Evaluation of each 
applicant's suitability will be made through records of accomplishments in the applicant's CV, selected publications, 
and references. 

Applicants should submit a CV, links to selected publications, and the contact information for three 
evaluators  to (email is preferred):
