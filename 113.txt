Job Description:
ERASMUS MUNDUS JOINT DOCTORATE in 
INFORMATION TECHNOLOGIES for BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE (IT4BI-DC) 
http://it4bi-dc.ulb.ac.be/

The Erasmus Mundus programme provides EU-funded SCHOLARSHIPS to students 
who have been selected by the consortium. 

The estimated net salary is €1400 - €2200. It varies across the institutions, 
among others due to variation in tax levels. 

More details concerning funds are available at
http://it4bi-dc.ulb.ac.be/fellowships_funds 

The IT4BI-DC Consortium is composed:
- Universite Libre de Bruxelles (ULB) (Belgium)
- Aalborg Universitet (AAU) (Denmark)
- Technische Universitaet Dresden (TUD) (Germany)
- Universitat Politecnica de Catalunya (UPC) (Spain)
- Poznan University of Technology (PUT) (Poland)
