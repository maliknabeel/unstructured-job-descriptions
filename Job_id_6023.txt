description: About the Role & Responsibilities
 
We seek an Executive Assistant to support the Editor-in Chief-and CEO of TechCrunch as well as general organizational needs of the business. Prefer an executive, C-level assistant who can handle 60% office administration and 20% special projects, and 20% personal needs of the executives. Environment is very busy, and we seek someone who doesn't crack under pressure from multi tasking. Must also be very computer and internet savvy (Mac), have good office and interpersonal skills with a polished demeanor and sense of decorum as you will interface with the most prominent names in Silicon Valley and tech industry  and members of the press. Ideal candidate will be an excellent organizer and muti-tasker and trustworthy. For the Founder, you will keep him scheduled, well-informed and on top of every situation. For the CEO you will be able to anticipate and handle business-oriented tasks and projects. For the Office, you will be the hub of information, keeping everything organized and operating smooth and efficiently.

Candidate must love technology and media as there is a TV studio and live blogging in the office. Duties will include scheduling, managing calendars, interfacing with all business executives and other tech personalities, coordinating in-office happenings, as well as handling office tasks such as receiving guests, vendor management, mail/courier needs, etc. Role is fast-paced and there is occasional travel. Salary is DOE. Candidates should have high energy and attention to detail and love being involved in a million projects at once.  Strong computer and internet skills.  Strong resarch and communication skills.
