description: The Product Manager develops plans and oversees implementation of new features and
enhancements (large and small) to the About.com website. The selected candidate should be a positive and active leader, and effective in communicating across technical and non-technical
departments. The qualified applicant will have the ability to plan and execute world-class
products with strong analytical follow-up.

Representative Responsibilities Include:
- Maintain and enhance the value proposition to About.com website users in order to increase traffic, repeat visits, and profitability
- Manage existing products and develop new products for the company based on strategic priorities, industry experience, idea generation, company technological capability, staff resources, and site and marketplace research.
- Manage new product development from strategic planning to implementation and post-launch analysis by working with editorial, marketing, sales, technology and finance.
- Define and document functional requirements
- Ensure projects are delivered on schedule and with high quality results.
- Optimize and analyze product performance and respond quickly to diagnose and correct any problems
- Remain abreast of competitors and trends in website design and functionality, integrating new technologies into the About.com site as they emerge

Qualifications:
The position requires expertise in website management and business principles combined with an understanding of consumer needs and technology. Specific requirements include:
- Three (3) or more years of experience in Internet-related product management or website development
- Five (5) or more years of total experience in marketing, technology or product development roles
- Experience leading website development projects: defining and communicating specifications, managing resources, leading cross-functional teams and managing ontime
implementation
- Experience product managing software application builds and 3rd party software implementations
- Superior ability to manage short- and long-term projects simultaneously, trading-off resources to achieve company objectives
- Skilled in standard software project and project management concepts, practices, and
procedures
- Knowledge of design principles and best practices in user experience design, website navigation, information architecture, and traffic retention.
- Experience in creating functional requirements and product specification documents
- Strong familiarity with Internet technology and current web trends, including social media and mobile
- Experience working on advertising products
- Exceptional troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent written and oral communication skills
- Bachelor's degree
